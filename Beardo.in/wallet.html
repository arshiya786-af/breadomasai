FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

+

How do I add BEARDOCASH to my Beardo Wallet?

You cannot add BEARDOCASH to your Beardo Wallet. You can only earn it via your purchase (If a CASHBACK offer is applicable on that order/product).

How can I check BEARDOCASH in my Beardo Wallet?

You can check your BEARDOCASH by following steps: 1. Login to your account & select "My Account" 2. Select "My Beardo Wallet"

Can my BEARDOCASH expire?

The BEARDOCASH you earned on an order has a validity of 4 months, it will be issued 48hrs after your order gets delivered. You will be notified via sms & email.

Can I withdraw and transfer BEARDOCASH to my bank account?

You cannot withdraw BEARDOCASH from your Beardo Wallet to your bank account. It can only be used to purchase products on Beardo.in.

Where would I receive refund if I placed an order through Beardo Wallet?

The refund amount will be reversed according to the payment method used for the said transaction.

Can I use BEARDOCASH on other ecommerce sites?

No. BEARDOCASH can't be used outside Beardo.in.

I placed order but didn't receive BEARDOCASH as promised.

Please note that BEARDOCASH is credited after the delivery of the products. You can contact our customer support team if you haven't received BEARDOCASH after the delivery of your order.

Why cant I use BEARDOCASH on my order?

BEARDOCASH can be redeemed on certain products only, please check if the products in your order are eligible for redemption.
